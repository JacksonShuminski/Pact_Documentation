Character Abilities

Character Abilities are defined by a Structure. This structure is used in the Base Character for creating Projectiles. It is used in Projectiles for describing projectile upgrades. It is used by the Enemy BP to determine the available abilities (light attack, heavy attack, dash) and relevant statistics (range, damage, ect.) of an enemy.

The Abilities Structure is made up of booleans to determine if an enemy can light attack, heavy attack, or dodge on the top layer. It also has a Light Abilities structure, Heavy Abilities structure, and Dash Abilities structure.

Light Abilities

The damage, fire rate, range, shot speed, and Light Upgrades structure are all stored in the Light Abilities structure. These statistics are used in the Create Projectile method in Base Character, and they are used in Base Projectile for dealing damage to other characters. The Enemy AI and Player BP also use some statistics from here life fire rate.

Heavy Abilities

The damage, fire rate, range, shot speed, and Heavy Upgrades structure are all stored in the Heavy Abilities structure. These statistics are used in the Create Projectile method in Base Character, and they are used in Base Projectile for dealing damage to other characters. The Enemy AI and Player BP also use some statistics from here life fire rate.

Dash Abilities

The Dash Strength and cooldown are both stored here, as well as the Dash Upgrades structure. The cooldown and strength are used for the player and enemy when they dash.
